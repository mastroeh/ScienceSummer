Mobile Setup Instructions

In the Bicycle Controller Script, comment out the lines before //***MOBILE CONTROLS****// and uncomment the lines after //***MOBILE CONTROLS****// 
To comment or uncomment code, highlight the text and press Ctrl + /

Set the game screen resolution to 1920x1080 or the resolution of your device.

You can control the on screen joystick with touch or mouse. 
Additional settings are present at the bottom of the bicycle controller script in the inspector.

Build the project to test out the controls.
